About Us

Intelog is an emerging supply chain & business management consultancy to solve the complex challenges in existing supply chain & business management process and create best & competitive solutions to support organizations to focus on core business & enabling their brands become part of the popular culture across the world and engage with consumers in their daily lives.

Intelog applies its renowned functional & operational expertise to provide best-in-class services across its integrated worldwide network.
